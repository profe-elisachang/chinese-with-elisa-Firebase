练习 1
选择题：选择正确的疑问句。
(Multiple choice: Choose the correct question sentence.)
A: 你想去"新世界"商店吗？
B: 是的。
A: 你是想买水果______买生活用品？
1. 还是
2. 和
3. 也
4. 再
+ 显示答案
正确答案：1. 还是

"还是"用于选择疑问句，询问两个选项中的一个。这里询问买什么东西，所以用"还是"。
"还是" (háishì) is used in alternative questions to ask between two or more options. Here, the question is asking which thing to buy, so "还是" is used.

"一...就..."表示两个动作紧接着发生。选项2完整地展示了这个结构。

练习 3
填空题：用"一...就..."完成句子。
(Fill in the blank with "一...就..." to complete the sentence.)
1. 他______到北京______学习汉语。
2. 我______有钱______买运动衣。
3. 孩子______毕业______当研究员。
4. 她______饿______去食堂。
+ 显示答案
1. 他一到北京就学习汉语。
Direct: As soon as he arrives in Beijing, he studies Chinese.
Natural: As soon as he arrived in Beijing, he started learning Chinese.

2. 我一有钱就买运动衣。
Direct: As soon as I have money, I buy sportswear.
Natural: Whenever I have money, I buy sportswear.

3. 孩子一毕业就当研究员。
Direct: As soon as the child graduates, he becomes a researcher.
Natural: As soon as the child graduated, he became a researcher.

4. 她一饿就去食堂。
Direct: As soon as she is hungry, she goes to the cafeteria.
Natural: Whenever she gets hungry, she goes to the cafeteria.

练习 1
选择题：选择正确的一项。
(Multiple choice: Choose the correct option.)
这些东西太贵了，我______。

1. 买得
2. 买不了
3. 买不到
4. 买得了
+ 显示答案
正确答案：2. 买不了

"V+不+了"表示没能力做某事。这里表示因为太贵而无法购买。
"V+不+了" (V+bù+le) means someone doesn't have the ability to do something. Here, it means they can't buy it because it's too expensive.

(Which sentence is a rhetorical question?)
1. 你上哪儿去啊？
2. 吃饭了吗？
3. 谁不想成绩好啊！
4. 您贵姓？
+ 显示答案
正确答案：3. 谁不想成绩好啊！

"反问句用否定的形式表达肯定的意思。这个句子用来强调大家都想成绩好。
A rhetorical question uses a negative form to express a positive meaning. This sentence is used to emphasize that everyone wants to do well.
