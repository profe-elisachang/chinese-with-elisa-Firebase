语法点

“多 + 形容词”结构：
“多”放在形容词前用来询问程度，如长度、高度、年龄等。例如：
"多" is placed before an adjective to ask about the degree of something (such as length, height, age, etc.). For example:
你家离学校有多远？
Nǐ jiā lí xuéxiào yǒu duō yuǎn?
How far is your home from the school?

常见问法：
Common questions:
多大 (how big/how old), 多长 (how long), 多高 (how tall/high), 多重zhòng (how heavy), 多宽kuān (how wide), 多远 (how far)

  • 他(有)多高？
    Tā duō gāo?
    How tall is he?
  • 这里的楼有多高？
    Zhèlǐ de lóu yǒu duō gāo?
    How tall are the buildings here?

例句

"有" + quantitative structure (+length, width, height, weight, distance)
    问句 答句
    你的孩子今年(有)多大？
    Nǐ de háizi jīnnián (yǒu) duōdà?
    How old is your child this year?     		他今年五岁。
    Tā jīnnián wǔ suì.
    He is five years old this year.
    这条河(多)长？
    Zhè tiáo hé (duō) cháng?
    How long is this river?     		这条河(有)三百（多）公里长。
    Zhè tiáo hé (yǒu) sānbǎi (duō) kǒnglǐ cháng.
    This river is 300 kilometers long.
    这座楼(多)高？
    Zhè zuò lóu (duō) gāo?
    How tall is this building?     		这座楼(有)五十米(多)高。
    Zhè zuò lóu (yǒu) wǔshí mǐ (duō) gāo.
    This building is 50 meters tall.
    这个箱子(多)重？
    Zhè ge xiāngzi (duō) zhòng?
    How heavy is this box?     		这个箱子(有)十公斤(多)重。
    Zhè ge xiāngzi (yǒu) shí gōngjīn (duō) zhòng.
    This box is ten kilograms.
    机场离这里(多)远？
    Jīchǎng lí zhèlǐ (duō) yuǎn?
    How far is the airport?     		机场离这里二十公里远。
    Jīchǎng lí zhèlǐ èrshí gōnglǐ yuǎn.
    The airport is 20 kilometers away.
